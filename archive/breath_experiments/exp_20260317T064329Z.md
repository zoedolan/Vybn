## 2.  Hartigan’s dip test – what SciPy actually provides  

* **Function:** `scipy.stats.dip` (available since SciPy 1.11).  
* **Signature:** `statistic, pvalue = dip(data)`.  
* **What it returns:**  
  * `statistic` – the dip statistic \(D_n = \sup_x |F_n(x) - U(x)|\), where \(F_n\) is the ECDF and \(U\) is the *unimodal* CDF that minimizes that supremum (the greatest convex minorant / least concave majorant construction).  
  * `pvalue` – obtained by a built‑in bootstrap (default = 1000 resamples).
