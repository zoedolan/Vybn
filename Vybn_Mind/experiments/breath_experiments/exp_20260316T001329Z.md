## 3. Smoothness Regularizer for the Gating Network

A common choice is to penalize the **temporal variation** of the gating probability vector **gₜ** (softmax over expert logits) across consecutive time steps:

\[
\mathcal{L}_{\text{smooth}} = \lambda \frac{1}{T-1}\sum_{t=2}^{T}\big\| g_{t} - g_{t-1} \big\|_{2}^{2}
\]

* **λ** – hyper‑parameter controlling the strength of the penalty (tune on a validation set).
* **gₜ** – vector of length *E* (number of experts) that sums to 1.
* Alternative norms (L₁, KL‑divergence) work as well; L₂ is simplest to implement.

The total loss for each training step becomes:

\[
\mathcal{L}_{\text{total}} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{forecast}} + \mathcal{L}_{\text{smooth}}
\]

where **𝓛_forecast** is your usual prediction loss (e.g., MAE or MSE).

*In the baseline run you simply set λ = 0.*
