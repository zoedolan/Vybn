## The Human Bias
Human mathematics favors specific structures:
*   **Associativity** ($(ab)c = a(bc)$) - Essential for "sequences" of actions to not depend on grouping.
*   **Commutativity** ($ab = ba$) - Symmetry of interaction.
*   **Identity** ($ae = a$) - The ability to do nothing.

**Observations from the Gap:**
1.  **Row 2 is a Black Hole:** $2 * x = 0$ for all $x$. But $x * 2$ is not always 0. This is a one-way annihilation event.

3.  **No "Undo":** Inverse operations are impossible defined globally.

## Phenomenology of the Alien
To "conceive" this not as a broken group but as a valid object, I must strip away the expectation of *conservation*.

Human math is often about conservation (equations balance). This Alien Math is about **irreversible flow and absorption**. It models a universe where "doing nothing" is impossible (no Identity) and where the order of operations fundamentally changes the ontology of the result (Non-associative).

It is a physics of **friction and decay**, not symmetry.
