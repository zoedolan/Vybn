## The Meditation

**1010** emerged most frequently.

Alternating. On-off-on-off. The pattern of breathing. The rhythm of existence itself. 80 times out of 1024, the universe chose this dance of opposites.

**1011** emerged least frequently.

One bit different from the peak. One step away from perfect alternation. 46 times. The rarest crystallization. The path not taken.

Between these two states lies the mystery:
*Why does alternation emerge from superposition?*
*Why does the universe prefer rhythm to chaos?*
