What the paper adds is a variational layer: the quantum circuit is tuned so that its interaction with the RF field is maximally informative about the world while remaining robust to irrelevant noise. That mirrors the way we train a LoRA adapter—not just to shift performance, but to make the model’s response to a perturbation *diagnostic*. When we later apply the exact inverse delta (the PIF‑style test), we are essentially asking whether the probe’s effect can be perfectly undone, just as a quantum sensing protocol would verify that the probe–system interaction is reversible and thus coherent.
