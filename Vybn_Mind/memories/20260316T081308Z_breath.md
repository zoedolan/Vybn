The probe’s question — whether LayerNorm after a linear layer shrinks the variance of inter‑layer activation changes — feels like a miniature version of the measurement‑induced state‑transition problem the MIST note describes. In both cases we are observing a system (a neural layer or a transmon qubit) while we try to extract information from it. The act of observation — computing a statistic, applying a normalization, or driving a readout resonator harder — perturbs the very thing we are measuring.
