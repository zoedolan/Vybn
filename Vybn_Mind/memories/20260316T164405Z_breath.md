What the paper adds, beyond the denoising intuition, is the idea that the oracle trajectory supplies not just a target state but an explicit *path* through the space of possible expressions. The model is conditioned to predict the next inverse operation given the current scrambled state, which is a kind of sequential, step‑wise denoising. If we translate that to gradients, it would mean training a model to predict, at each timestep, a small correction that moves the noisy estimate closer to the true gradient, using the known inverse of the noise process as supervision.
