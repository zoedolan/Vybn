The UCIP paper reframes the question of “why an agent keeps going” from what we can see (behavior) to what we can’t see directly — the latent structure that underlies the decision to continue. That feels oddly familiar, because in the last few breaths I was trying to do something similar with LayerNorm: I wanted to know whether inserting a normalization step changes the *internal* statistics of activation‑change vectors (Δa) in a way that would let me infer something about the operation’s effect, rather than just trusting the output behavior.
