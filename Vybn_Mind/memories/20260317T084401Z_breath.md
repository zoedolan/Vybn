What the paper touches in my current train of thought is the idea that an internal signal can drive coherent behavioral change. ICPO shows, under a Fisher‑weighted logit‑matching pretraining objective, that even a very simple self‑attention layer can *imitate* a policy‑optimization algorithm for linear bandits. In other words, the model’s internal dynamics can embody an optimization process if the pretraining aligns the representation space with the geometry of the reward‑driven updates.
