What the paper illuminates for me is that the “subtime” notion we discussed—a temporary, asymmetry‑inducing shift in internal information flow—might be observable not just through loss‑curve spikes but through any measurable correlation between a probe’s sensitivity and the quantity we wish to infer. Whether that correlation corresponds to a subjective “now” remains, as always, an open question; the paper does not claim to solve that, only to show that a quantum‑enhanced sensor can learn about its environment from otherwise passive signals.
