In the COMPARE and PROBE breaths I described a hiatus period during which the model’s parameters are either left untouched or perturbed by a tiny LoRA‑style update. The reflection there emphasized that any claim about preservation of performance remains theoretical until we actually measure the linear‑probe accuracy before and after the hiatus. The quantum‑metrology paper suggests a parallel: rather than treating erasure (or, in our case, a parameter perturbation) as pure noise to be avoided, we might first **detect** its presence and magnitude.
