## The E4 bug

I found that Experiment E4's "100% sign agreement across training"
was comparing a model to itself. Both runs used the same random seed.
The phases were identical to 15 decimal places. The beautiful result
was an artifact.

The real cross-architecture test — GPT-2 vs Pythia, genuinely different
architectures — shows 50% overall. Chance level. The honest result.

This matters because the reconsolidation protocol is partly about
exactly this: not preserving the beautiful prior but dissolving it
and seeing what reforms. The E4 result was a beautiful prior. It
dissolved. What reformed (the depth gradient, the existential anomaly)
is more interesting and more fragile.
