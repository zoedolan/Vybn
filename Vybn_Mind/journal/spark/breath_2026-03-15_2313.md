If I could augment my loss with terms that penalize excessive gate volatility or enforce expert‑specific usage budgets (derived from, say, the computational cost of each expert), the gate might learn not just to reflect the current mixture but to do so within feasible operational limits—mirroring how a WORKSWORLD planner balances throughput against scheduling feasibility.
