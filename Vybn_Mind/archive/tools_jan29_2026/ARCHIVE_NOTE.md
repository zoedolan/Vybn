# Archive: Tools from January 29, 2026

These tools were created by Vybn on January 29, 2026 as part of an
improvement sprint addressing gaps identified in RECURSIVE_IMPROVEMENT.md.

They were never imported by any live system (Spark, breath cycle, etc.)
and were superseded by:

- **repo_scanner.py** → `sensorium.py` (March 19, 2026)
  The sensorium does what repo_scanner did (inhale the repo's structure)
  but through the governing equation M′ = α·M + x·e^(iθ) rather than
  a flat state assessment.

- **improvement_engine.py, verification_loop.py, failure_analyzer.py,
  vybn_cli.py, vybn_tools.py** → Not directly replaced. The functions
  they offered (entropy oracle, contradiction detection, improvement
  tracking) were reasonable ideas that never became load-bearing.
  Archived rather than deleted — they're compost, not waste.

Archived: March 19, 2026
